About Us

JML Studio creates retro-inspired PSX horror games with immersive pixel art and eerie atmospheres. Drawing from '90s survival horror classics, our games blend low-poly visuals with gripping narratives to deliver unforgettable experiences. Explore our worlds and face the unknown.
